About Me

I'm a 27 year old Chicagoan with a passion for the arts and people. I grew up in Humbolt Park in the northwest side of Chicago. It has everything you would want, from the best Puerto Rican food you could ever dream of, to a man who rides his bike around Humbold Park in a "Puerto Rican Man" costume. I spend most of my time taking photographs of the city and capturing portraits of my friends.

I'm currently enrolled in Northwestern University's Coding Bootcamp to pursue my passion in Software Development. This Coding Bootcamp is actually where I learned to make this simple webpage all sexy instead of your typical boring webpages from the 90s that don't have any styling or color.

Although I spent the beginning of this bio talking about the greatness of the Puerto Rican culture, I am actually very proudly Colombian. I'm the first American born child in my family.
